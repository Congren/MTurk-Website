Help analyze important pictures

We are conducting a research study on how accurately MTurk users can identify pictures compared to their counterparts on other crowdsource platforms. The better you do the more chances for additional compensation!

We believe it is possible to achieve an up to 70% accuracy rate, and further compensation will be provided if the rate is actually higher! We'll be splitting a 10 dollar pot between workers who participated in the winning platform.

For every picture that the user gets right, we will be compensating the user with 3 cents, with up to a dollar extra compensation depending on whether or not the answer to the last question is accurate. Thank you for your participation!

Study Instructions

You will be trying to determine how many people are in each picture.

A person counts as long as you can see any part of their body.

You will be compensated with 3 cents per correct answer, with 10 cents for the second to last picture and an extra dollar for a correct answer on the last picture.
